Hey, I’m Jesse.

I live in Los Angeles.

Background

I grew up in Oak Ridge, TN and Princeton, NJ and currently live in Los Angeles, CA. For the last two and half years, I have been working on a global peace initiative with filmmaker David Lynch and his nonprofit. Before that, I worked with an urban education nonprofit in Newark, NJ helping expand equal opportunity through high quality education.

As a musician, I have worked and collaborated with a variety of artists and media outlets ranging from Animal Collective to Vogue Paris. I have performed at festivals like All Tomorrow's Parties UK, Sasquatch!, and Midi Festival in France and at venues like J. Paul Getty Museum and Bowery Ballroom NYC. I have also had artwork hosted by spaces such as Big Medium Gallery in Austin, TX and The Hunter Museum of American Art in Chattanooga, TN.

I've been tinkering with different types of code - HTML, CSS, Pure Data, and a little Javascript - for years and am really excited and eager to put my full attention on becoming an expert web developer. In addition to eventually building cool, interactive websites, I hope to one day create tools that help empower and educate people through digital technology.

Goals

In 2016, I plan to become an expert in:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • AngularJS
  • Node.js

Eventually, I’d like to work for a web design firm helping clients fully realize their brand potential online. I hope to be able to volunteer my skills to nonprofits, family and friends who need help creating websites. I also want to learn to build applications with Javascript to help existing systems in the workplace become more effective, efficient and fun.
